## The Novel in Brief

A British mathematician, **Adam Gladstone**, discovers that his late father-in-law William McAteer's five published histories of the Seychelles inadvertently map the entrance to a pirate's underground complex. He races against Russian AI billionaire **Viktor Volkov** — who has superior technology but is solving the wrong problem (the La Buse cipher, which only navigates the *interior*, not the entrance). The final key to the inner chamber is held by Adam's daughter **Aimee**: a Seychellois Creole childhood rhyme whose physical sequence her body remembers but no AI can recover from text alone.

**Central theme:** Data vs. understanding. Viktor's AI knows more. Aimee's embodied memory wins.

---

## The Three-Layer Mystery (Core Architecture)

This is the structural spine of the novel. Never confuse the layers:

1. **Layer 1 — The Entrance:** Solved by Adam via cross-referencing McAteer's five books. The La Buse cipher does NOT reveal the entrance. This subverts the genre.
2. **Layer 2 — Interior navigation:** Solved jointly by Viktor (60%, via AI cipher analysis) and Adam (30%, cultural/linguistic interpretation). The cipher only works *inside*.
3. **Layer 3 — The inner chamber:** Solved by Aimee via recognition of the Creole children's rhyme *Ti chat ki pe dòmi* — she doesn't decode it, she *performs* it from embodied memory.

## Writing Rules (from the Style Guide)

- **POV:** Close third person. No omniscient narration. Stay in one character's head per scene.
- **Tense:** Past tense throughout.
- **SYBIL outputs** use a specific format: `[SYBIL: Analysis complete. Confidence: 87%. Interpretation: insufficient contextual data.]`
- **Creole speech:** Suggest through rhythm and vocabulary — never phonetic spelling.
- **Chapter length:** 2,500–4,000 words. Every chapter ends on a hook or a turn.
- **The Seychelles:** Active setting, not backdrop. Specific sensory detail; avoid brochure writing.
- **Viktor:** Never cartoonishly villainous. He must be argued with, not sneered at. He is right about things.
- **Aimee:** Must contribute in every act — her climactic door scene must feel earned, not sudden.
- **The thesis** (data vs. embodied understanding) is never stated directly. Only shown.
